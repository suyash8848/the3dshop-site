How it's made

Four steps, one printer, no factory line.

Every order is queued individually — there's no warehouse stock, so lead time is part of the deal, not a delay.

01

Design & slice — model checked, oriented and sliced for the least visible seam.

02

Print in small batches — 1 to 6 units per run, so colour and finish stay consistent.

03

Hand-finish & QC — supports removed, edges cleaned, every piece checked before packing.

04

Ship on request — packed and sent once you've confirmed size, colour and address over DM.

About the shop

Small studio, precise machines.

The 3D Shop is a one-printer studio based in Pune, India. We design and print small, useful objects — not novelty prints — and finish every piece by hand before it leaves the bench.

There's no storefront checkout here on purpose. Every piece is made to order, so sizing, colour and small customisations are worked out over a DM before anything goes on the print bed.
